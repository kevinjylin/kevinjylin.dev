Figuring out what was actually happening at UCR meant checking a dozen Instagram accounts, a
few flyers, and whatever group chat happened to mention it, and I still missed half of it.
Highlander Hub pulls every campus event into one place so students can find what's going on
without the scavenger hunt.

It started as a "why doesn't this already exist?" question, which is usually how my projects start.
