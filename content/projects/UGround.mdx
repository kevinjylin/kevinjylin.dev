I kept finding out about concert presales the day after they ended. By the time the
announcement reached me, the cheap tickets were already gone and resale prices had usually
doubled. UGround watches for presales from the artists you follow and pings you the moment
one goes live, so you and your friends actually catch the early price.

I built it for myself first, but it turns out plenty of people miss presales the exact same way.
